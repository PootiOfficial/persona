The Palaces in Persona 5 Royal

Palace 1: Castle

Kamoshidas Palace

Palace-ruler: Suguru Kamoshida

Distortion: Castle of Lust

The Shujin Academy, which appears in Kamoshida's perception as his personal castle in which he rules as king.

Important Items:

  • Golden medal
  • Volleyball key

Boss:

Asmodeus (Kamoshidas Shadow)

Palace 2: Museum

Madarames Palace

Palace-ruler: Ichiryusai Madarame

Distortion: Museum of Vanity

Madarame's house, which he perceives as an opulent museum in which his "works of art" are exhibited.

Important Items:

  • Painting key
  • Laser cruiser

Boss:

Azazel (Madarames Shadow)

Palace 3: Flying Bank

Kaneshiros Palace

Palast-ruler: Junya Kaneshiro

Distortion: Bank of Gluttony

A massive bank hovering over Shibuya, viewing the city as its personal source of money.

Important Items:

  • PIN-Numbers
  • Bank key

Boss:

Bael (Kaneshiros Shadow)

Palace 4: Pyramid

Futabas Palace

Palace-ruler: Futaba Sakura

Distortion: Pyramid of Wrath

An Egyptian pyramid in a desert, symbolizing Futaba's isolation and self-blame.

Important Items:

  • Anubis-key
  • Sacred Treasure Map

Boss:

Sphinx (Futabas cognitive self)

Palace 5: Space station

Okumuras Palace

Palace-ruler: Kunikazu Okumura

Distortion: Spaceport of Greed

A futuristic space station that represents Okumura's ambitions and his treatment of his employees as robots.

Important Items:

  • Biometric card
  • MDL-Core

Boss:

Mammon (Okumuras Shadow)

Palace 6: Casino

Sae's Palace

Palace-ruler: Sae Niijima

Distortion: Casino of Envy/Jealousy

A glitzy casino that represents Sae's view that life is an unfair game of chance and that the justice system is rigged.

Important Items:

  • game chips
  • High security key

Boss:

Leviathan (Saes Shadow)

Palace 7: Cruise ship

Shido's Palace

Palace-ruler: Masayoshi Shido

Distortion: Cruiser of Pride

A gigantic cruise ship sailing through a sunken Japan symbolizes Shido's indifference to the country as he sets course for his own future.

Important Items:

  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Captain's Key

Boss:

Samael (Shidos Shadow)

Palace 8: Mementos Tiefen

Mementos Depths

Palace-ruler: Collective unconscious of humanity

Distortion: Prison of Sloth/Regression/Acedia

The deepest levels of Mementos, revealing the prison of regression - a symbol of humanity's desire to be controlled.

Important Items:

  • Prison-Key
  • Holy Chalice

Boss:

Yaldabaoth (God of control)

Palace 9: Laboratory of Happiness

Maruki's Palace

Palace-ruler: Takuto Maruki

Distortion: Center of Melancholy/Laboratory of Sorrow

A shiny, futuristic research center that represents Maruki's desire to create a reality where everyone is happy.

Important Items:

  • ID-Card
  • Stem cells

Boss:

Azathoth/Adam Kadmon (Marukis Shadow)
